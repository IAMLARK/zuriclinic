SERVICES

At Zuri Hospital, we are committed to providing a diverse range of high-quality healthcare services to meet the unique needs of our patients. Our team of dedicated healthcare professionals is here to ensure that you receive expert care with compassion and precision. Explore the comprehensive services offered at Zuri Hospital:

State of the Art Equipment

Zuri Hospital prides itself on having state-of-the-art medical facilities equipped with the latest advancements in healthcare technology

Emergency Help

Zuri Hospital prioritizes the well-being of our community by offering round-the-clock emergency services. Our dedicated emergency response team is trained to provide swift and efficient assistance in critical situations

Enriched Pharmacy

We understand the importance of a well-stocked and enriched pharmacy. Our pharmacy is not just a dispensary but a comprehensive resource for a wide range of pharmaceutical needs. Patients can rely on us for access to a diverse selection of medications, including specialized and rare drugs

Affordable Healthcare

We understand the financial challenges associated with medical care, and as such, we strive to provide affordable healthcare solutions through transparent pricing and cost-effective treatment plans

Our Main Services include:

  • Emergency Services: Our Emergency Department is ready 24/7 to provide immediate and life-saving care for critical medical conditions and emergencies
  • Medical and Surgical Care: Zuri Hospital offers general medical and surgical services for a variety of health conditions.
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU): For patients requiring advanced monitoring and critical care, our Intensive Care Unit is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by skilled professionals to ensure the highest level of care.
  • Pediatrics: Zuri Hospital understands the unique healthcare needs of our youngest patients
  • Imaging and Diagnostic Services: Our Imaging Department utilizes advanced technology to perform a variety of diagnostic tests, including X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and more
  • Surgical Services: Zuri Hospital's state-of-the-art Operating Rooms are staffed by skilled surgeons and equipped with advanced technology
  • Rehabilitation Services: Our Rehabilitation Department offers physical therapy, occupational therapy, and other rehabilitation services to support patients in regaining optimal function and mobility after illness, injury, or surgery.
  • Maternity and Birthing Services: Experience comprehensive maternity care at Zuri Hospital. Our team provides prenatal, delivery, and postpartum care with a focus on the well-being of both mother and baby.
  • Mental Health Services: Zuri Hospital recognizes the importance of mental health in overall well-being. Our mental health services encompass counseling, therapy, and psychiatric care to support individuals facing mental health challenges
  • Specialized Clinics: Zuri Hospital hosts specialized clinics catering to specific medical conditions and needs. These clinics provide focused care and expertise in areas such as cardiology, oncology, endocrinology, and more.
